Services

What EIC focuses on first.

Because we’re new, we’ve chosen a few service areas that are realistic for a student team and genuinely helpful for partners. All work is done pro bono.

Strategy support for small teams & organizations

Help small teams and organizations get clear on what they’re trying to do this term.

  • Goal-setting for the semester or year
  • Simple roadmaps & initiative planning
  • Event or program planning support

Lightweight research & insights

Basic research and simple analysis to support decisions.

  • Desk research & competitor scans
  • Surveys / feedback summaries
  • Short recommendation decks or memos

Operations & process clean-up

Helping small teams make their workflows less chaotic.

  • Mapping “how things work now”
  • Identifying pain points & bottlenecks
  • Suggesting simple process tweaks
Expectations

What partners can expect (and not expect).

What we aim to provide

  • Structured thinking and clear questions.
  • Honest timelines and regular check-ins.
  • Slides or docs you can actually use.

What we can’t promise (yet)

  • Big “consulting firm” style outcomes.
  • Deep technical or legal advice.
  • Perfection on the first try.
