A student association built to elevate impact for real organizations.

Elevate Impact Consulting (EIC) is a brand-new, student-led, pro bono consulting association. We partner with non-profits, small businesses, and community organizations while giving students a safe place to learn how consulting actually works.

At a glance

What Elevate Impact Consulting is about.

We’re building a space where students can practice consulting skills on real projects, and where organizations get thoughtful, structured support at no cost.

Student-led

Built and run by students who want more than just theory — they want to actually work on real problems.

Pro bono

We don’t charge fees. In return, we ask for partners who are patient and open to working with a learning-focused team.

Learning first

Our “win” is skill-building, not revenue. Everything is designed so members can talk about real experience in interviews.
