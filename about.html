About EIC

A student-made, pro bono consulting association.

Elevate Impact Consulting was created by students who wanted more than case competitions and theory. We wanted a place to practice real consulting work, with real organizations, in a way that still feels safe and learning-focused.

Our mission: give students the chance to develop real consulting skills — research, problem-structuring, communication, and teamwork — while helping clubs, non-profits, and small businesses move forward.


We don’t pretend to be a polished, long-running firm. We’re honest about being early, and we treat that as a strength: it means we’re flexible, hungry, and open to feedback.

Right now, EIC is:

  • A founding group working on our first projects.
  • Experimenting with simple processes and templates.
  • Looking for partners who value learning and collaboration.
Values

How we want EIC to feel.

Honest about our stage

We won’t fake experience we don’t have. We’re upfront that we’re early and learning.

Professional effort

Even though it’s student-run and free, we still aim to communicate clearly and show up prepared.

Growth mindset

Feedback isn’t personal — it’s how we refine our work and grow as a team.
