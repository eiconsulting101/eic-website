Join EIC

Grow with us from day one.

We’re building EIC from the ground up. If you’re curious about consulting, like working with people, and are okay with things being a bit scrappy at the start, this might be a good fit.

Who we’re looking for

  • Students who care about doing real work, not just collecting titles.
  • People comfortable asking questions and saying “I don’t know yet.”
  • Team players who actually follow through.

What you’ll practice

  • Breaking down messy problems into clear steps.
  • Doing research and turning it into useful slides.
  • Talking to partners in a professional way.

How to express interest

Use the form on the Contact page and mention that you’d like to join as a student member. We’ll follow up with next steps once we’re onboarding.
